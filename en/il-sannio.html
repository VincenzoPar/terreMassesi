Our story has its origins in Massa Di Faicchio, an ancient village embraced
by sunny hills, lush vineyards, small cellars carved into the tuff, and
centuries-old legends. Cradled by a gentle breeze,
Massa is for us a locus amenus, custodian of our tradition, witness
to the history of our family.
La famiglia Di Leone

THE HEART OF OUR WINE

The terroir of Terre Massesi is a unique mosaic of flavors, mitigated by the Mediterranean climate. In our vineyards, the volcanic soils blend harmoniously with the limestone to create an idyllic environment for grape cultivation. This is complemented by the art and millenary culture of Sannio viticulture that defines the unique and distinctive character of our wines.
