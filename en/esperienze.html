Pompei, from the Antiquity of the Vitae of the Wine and Food.

Sunday 23 and Monday 24 June 2024 Terre Massesi will participate in the event “Pompei, from the Antiquity of the Vitae of the Wine and Food” which will be held in the suggestive location of the historic Excavations of Pompeii. It will be a special occasion to discover Nevene, Lengualonga and Lucella, our DOP wines that will make you experience a unique sensory experience. You will be able to feel its fruity aroma, try its floral notes, and savor its authentic taste. Wine is culture, tradition, and life.
